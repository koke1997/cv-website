## Music Journey

My journey with music started in 2008 when I first discovered electronic music production. Over the years, I've developed my skills across various genres:

- **Ambient & Chill** - Atmospheric soundscapes for relaxation
- **Tech House** - Driving rhythms and groovy basslines
- **Electronic** - Experimental sounds and synthesis

## Creative Philosophy

I believe technology and art complement each other beautifully. My background in software engineering informs my creative work - I approach music production with the same analytical mindset I apply to code, while my creative pursuits help me think outside the box in technical challenges.

> "The intersection of technology and creativity is where the most interesting things happen."
